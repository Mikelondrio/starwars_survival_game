BOUNTY HUNTER

These enigmatic figures operate outside the bounds of conventional law, navigating the shadows of the galaxy with cunning and precision. JEDI

The Jedi are the guardians of peace and justice in the galaxy. They master the Force and harmonize with it. Although most people have a good opinion of them, there are still those who distrust their teachings.

NOBLE

A master of negotiation and agreements. An expert in dialectics, knows how to navigate the upper echelons and who to contact in the lower ones.

SOLDIER

Is a disciplined warrior trained in combat techniques and armed with advanced weaponry. Whether serving in the ranks of the Republic, Empire, or various factions, soldiers play a crucial role in battles and conflicts across the galaxy.

SITH

A Jedi who has let himself be carried away by his emotions and succumbed to the dark side. Incredibly powerful and influential, his powers rival those of his 'good' counterparts, although often his emotions make him unstable.

SCAVENGER

A resourceful individual who navigates the galaxy's remnants, seeking valuable salvage and lost treasures amidst the wreckage of starships and ancient civilizations. They often lead solitary lives, relying on their wits, agility, and knowledge of technology to survive in harsh environments.
